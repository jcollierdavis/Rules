# Rules
The Server's rules

1. Play nice
2. Respect other players and the server staff
3. Don't be a griefer.  Don't harass other players or their structures.
4. Don't cheat or abuse the game.
5. Only the GM can ban/unban players
  a. Players will not make decisions about who can and who can't play
6. Do not PvP another player without asking first
7. Avoid using World Edit to make large structures
  a. This creates lots of lag and can crash the server
8. Do not share the server IP
  a. The server isn't very powerfull and can only handle a few players at the same time
